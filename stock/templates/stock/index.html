How does it work?

The stock price simulator here works on the principle of Monte Carlo Simulations. It requires the ticker symbol for which the stock prices have to be predicted along with the required number of days of prediction. Moreover it can control the number of simulations (predictions). It predicts stock prices for future dates based on a probablity distribution computed using standard deviation of past data.
